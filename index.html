Why Waitomo?

Waitomo, the underground landscape

Don't look above ground, look below..tubing, abseiling, walking tours, boat tours, raft tours and more! In the western Waikato region of the North Island of New Zealand is the underground landscape of Waitomo boasting vast cave networks and glowworms. GO gently with a boat trip or go lively with a caving adventure and tubing trip

More than just caves...see a real-life kiwi bird at the nearby native bird park or take a half-day trip to the rugged coast off Waitomo to discover waterfalls, magnificent limestone arches and even New Zealand's hot water beach
